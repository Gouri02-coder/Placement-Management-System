💡 Preparation Tips

Consistent Practice

Solve at least 2-3 problems daily and review previously solved problems regularly.

Mock Interviews

Practice with peers or use platforms like Pramp for mock interview sessions.

Time Management

Practice solving problems within time constraints to simulate real interview conditions.

Conceptual Understanding

Focus on understanding underlying concepts rather than memorizing solutions.
